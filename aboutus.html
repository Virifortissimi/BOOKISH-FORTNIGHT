About GrassRootViews

GrassRootViews is a social platform that bridges the gap between various communities in a town/city and help spread details and information about changes, developments, situations and circumstances that are happening around people, as well as provide helplines to people in emergency situations. The information comes in the form of crowdsourcing the immediate happenings from people that are at the particular location where the news you are searching for are situated.

This initiative originated from a group of young intellectuals who saw information flow as a bit bias, where news reports and blog posts are only the catchy items, and thought to make issues arising from the general populace known. Also, no mediums to get the news in communities out to the people living around. For instance, Road blocks, fire outbreaks, traffic situation, robbery, registration centers and polling stations in communities, red alerts (Stay indoor situation), government projects and its updates, public disturbance (fights) etc., would be all provided to the users.
